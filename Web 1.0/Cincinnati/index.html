Omnimax Theatre as Product

As a revenue stream coming into the Greater Metropolitan City of Cincinnati if the Carl Lindner Omnimax Theatre was marketed to other cities. Not every city has an Omnimax and to get that Hollywood infrastructure where when a film is debuted it rakes in several hundred million dollars if not billion if the city of Cincinnati started itself off as a distribution cetner of Omnimax Theatres if we proactively approached cities about building film centerpieces and then went about the business of producing OMNIMAX content.

Bus

If the City of Cincinnati produced subway quality work in it's busing system if there was a network of buses that rivaled the ease of use of the car. If we are not going to continue work on the subway if we went about the business of automating the work of navigating buses and if we had a bus system that traveled 24/7 and bus drivers turned into light work security guards on the bus. If the buses just ran.

List of Cincinnati Businesses

Cincinnati has a major culmination of industries within it but we are weak in media. If noting this detailed profile of Cincinnati if Cincinnati went about developing itself and fleshing out a cadre of business for itself that dealt with media. We are always most proud of the work people come here to do never the work that we do here ourselves. We would have to study.

Mayoral Discussion Forum

There is currently to my knowledge with some searching no Facebook Group or Collectivist Discussion forum dedicated to the class of citizen known as Mayor or City Council Member. There are several hundreds mayors and several thousand council members and there is no online discussion forum for this class of people that is the reason we have a city.
